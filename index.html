Intro

Welcome to the exciting world of Dandibiyo, where sticks and stones have been replaced by sticks and a small piece of wood called a biyo. This traditional Nepali game has been played for centuries and is still enjoyed by many today. The game requires skill, teamwork, and a competitive spirit as players aim to knock over their opponent's biyo while defending their own. With its rich history and challenging gameplay, Dandibiyo is a game that's sure to keep you on the edge of your seat. So grab your dandi and biyo, assemble your team, and get ready for a thrilling game that's fun for all ages!
